Cookies

Last updated: 22 March 2026

TL;DR

We use essential cookies so the site works, plus optional analytics/marketing cookies if you say yes. You can change your choices anytime in Settings.

What are cookies?

Small files stored on your device to remember preferences, keep you logged in, and help us understand site usage.

Types we use

  1. Strictly necessary – required for core features (e.g., page navigation, session security).
  2. Performance/analytics – help us improve (page views, button clicks). Set only with consent.
  3. Functional – remember preferences like language.
  4. Marketing – measure campaigns or show relevant content. Set only with consent.

Managing cookies

  • Use our Settings panel to toggle categories.
  • You can also control cookies in your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect site features.

Examples of cookies we may set

  • session_id (essential) – keeps you logged in; expires when you close the browser.
  • consent_state (functional) – remembers your cookie choices; ~12 months.
  • analytics_* (analytics) – usage stats; up to ~13 months, only if you consent.
